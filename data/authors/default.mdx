The Interact Club is a student-led club that aims to help those in need and urge others to do the same. We've been bringing together like-minded students to raise cash and give back to the community since 2019.

We want to lend a hand to those who are in need, whether they are children in need of education or families in need of housing.

We collaborate closely with international organisations and do our utmost to assist them.
